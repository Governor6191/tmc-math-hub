About TMC Math Hub

TMC Math Hub is a free learning platform for KNUST Mathematics undergraduates. Every course's slides and textbooks from Year 1 to Year 4 are readable right here, streamed from the TMC academic board's Drive hub.

What's inside

Beyond the library itself: over ten thousand practice questions with instant marking and worked solutions, a mock exam hall with timed, Moodle-style trial papers so your first online mid-sem feels familiar rather than frightening, and in-browser coding labs where you write and run real Python and C, checked against tests, with nothing to install.

Your progress and privacy

You never need an account. By default everything you do (scores, streaks, exam attempts) is remembered on your own device only and uploaded nowhere.

Signing in with Google is optional. If you do, your learning progress syncs to your account so it follows you across devices. That data is stored in this project's Firebase database, readable only by you, and contains nothing but your question scores, streak days, exam attempts and lab scores. Sign out any time; your device copy stays with you.

Contributing

Spotted a missing course, a broken file, or a great YouTube lesson for a topic? Send it to the TMC academic board, or open an issue on this site's GitHub repository. New slides added to the Drive hub appear here after the next catalog refresh.

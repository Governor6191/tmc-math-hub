About TMC Math Hub

TMC Math Hub is a free learning platform for KNUST Mathematics undergraduates. Every course's slides and textbooks from Year 1 to Year 4 are readable right here, streamed from the TMC academic board's Drive hub.

What's coming

This is stage one of a bigger plan. Next come practice questions for every course (instant marking with worked solutions) and a mock exam hall: timed, Moodle-style trial exams so your first online mid-sem feels familiar, not frightening. No accounts, ever; your progress will be remembered on your own device.

Contributing

Spotted a missing course, a broken file, or a great YouTube lesson for a topic? Send it to the TMC academic board, or open an issue on this site's GitHub repository. New slides added to the Drive hub appear here after the next catalog refresh.

About TMC Math Hub

TMC Math Hub is a free learning platform for KNUST Mathematics undergraduates. Every course's slides and textbooks from Year 1 to Year 4 are readable right here, streamed from the TMC academic board's Drive hub.

What's inside

The library is only the start. Alongside the slides you get:

  • Practice questions: over ten thousand across every mathematics course in all four years, with instant marking, worked explanations, and fill-in-the-blank solutions that award credit step by step.
  • A mock exam hall: timed, Moodle-style trial papers with a question grid, flagging and a full review afterwards, so your first online mid-sem feels familiar rather than frightening.
  • Coding labs: write and run real Python and C in your browser, graded against tests, nothing to install. Courses with Python problems also put at least one coding question in every mock exam.
  • YouTube lessons: every topic links out to trusted channels for that subject, one click from the course page.
  • My progress: your study streak, accuracy and per-course mastery, tracked from your own work so you can watch yourself improve.

There is also a free AI study helper: after practice or a mock exam, ask it to explain any question you missed, or anything else you are stuck on, right on the page.

Your progress and privacy

You never need an account. By default everything you do (scores, streaks, exam attempts) is remembered on your own device only and uploaded nowhere.

Signing in with Google is optional. If you do, your learning progress syncs to your account so it follows you across devices. That data is stored in this project's Firebase database, readable only by you, and contains nothing but your question scores, streak days, exam attempts and lab scores. Sign out any time; your device copy stays with you.

Contributing

Spotted a wrong answer, a missing course, a broken file, or a great YouTube lesson we should add? The fastest route is the feedback page, where you can attach a screenshot. You can also open an issue on this site's GitHub repository. New slides added to the Drive hub appear here after the next catalog refresh.
